The AI Timeline

Key moments that shaped artificial intelligence

The Foundation

Alan Turing proposes the concept of machine intelligence and the famous "Turing Test"

1940s

Birth of AI

The Dartmouth Conference coins the term "Artificial Intelligence" and establishes it as a field of study

1956

Early Programs

First AI programs emerge: ELIZA, SHRDLU, and early expert systems show promise

1960s-70s

Expert Systems Boom

Commercial AI applications flourish with rule-based expert systems in various industries

1980s

Machine Learning Rise

Statistical approaches gain prominence. Deep Blue defeats Garry Kasparov in chess

1990s-2000s

Deep Learning Revolution

Neural networks achieve breakthroughs in image recognition, natural language processing, and game playing

2010s

Generative AI Era

Large language models and generative AI transform how we interact with artificial intelligence
